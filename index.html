MEET FARMER TOM

Tom has been making ice cream for over 20 years, using milk from his own herd of Jersey cows. What started as a small farm operation has grown into an award-winning ice cream business, but Tom's commitment to quality and traditional methods remains unchanged.

Every morning, Tom is up before dawn tending to the herd. "The secret to great ice cream is happy cows," he says with a smile. "When you treat your animals well and use the freshest ingredients, you can taste the difference in every scoop."

Tom's passion for ice cream is matched only by his love for the land. Sustainability and animal welfare are at the heart of everything he does, ensuring that Farmer Tom's Ice Cream is not just delicious, but ethically made.
