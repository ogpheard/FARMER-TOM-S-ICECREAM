ABOUT US

At Farmer Tom's, we believe exceptional ice cream starts with exceptional ingredients. Every batch is handcrafted using fresh milk from our own herd and local farms, creating flavors that honor tradition while embracing creativity.

Our commitment to quality and sustainability has earned recognition across the industry, but our greatest reward is the joy we bring to every scoop. From our family farm to your table, we take pride in every step of the journey.
